Family Owned and Operated

Located on Shelby Street at Boardway, Clarksdale TV has been apart of Louisville since 1938, we service all makes and models of TVs. We also rent televisions for special occasions and events.

Clarksdale's History

In 1938 Joe Koch opened a radio repair shop at 706 Shelby Street. Around 1944, his brother Larry, returning home after being in the Marines, join Joe were they built the business to include repairing TV's.

The brothers ran their business until the 1980's when Larry's son Donnie Koch took over the shop. Clarksdale TV is the oldest TV repair shop in Louisvile and is still operating from the same location, since 1938.

Repair
